Wow!! I have a story

It all begins in 2013, I was in planning mode. I had finished my degree and worked for couple of years when I decided to make that bold move to migrate to the United States from South London United Kingdom, at first, I was frighten Moving to a new country on my own.

With every last goodbye drawing my departure closer and closer, I tend to wonder why I was leaving everyone that ever loved me to go to a place where this people won’t be

Before I left England, I knew I was extremely quick at finding patterns and at mental calculations, I was great at math’s, I loved maps, and charts, and graphs, but I could see no place where I could use these talents even though my first Degree was in manufacturing Engineering

On getting to the United States, I immediately enrolled at a university to do a master degree so I can follow my passion for Data because I strongly believe that data when organized and manipulated carefully, with a mix of the right technologies, can create a lot of impact on society.
